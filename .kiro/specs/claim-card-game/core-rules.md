### **Phase 1: Initial Declaration (Simultaneous)**

All players simultaneously decide whether to declare a suit:
- **Declare:** Choose a suit and optionally place one or more face-up cards of that suit from their hand
- **Don't Declare:** Take an immediate penalty based on the value of their highest card

Players who don't declare accumulate penalties each round until they declare.

**Timing:** A 30-second countdown is used for players to make their decisions. After the countdown, there is a "3, 2, 1" count with a 1-second pause after each number to allow players to simultaneously reveal their declaration or place cards. This brief pause ensures all players have time to act without reading instructions or making last-minute decisions.

### **Phase 2: Floor Card Rounds**

After the initial declaration, the game proceeds in rounds:

1. **Dealer Reveal:** The dealer places one card face-up as the **Floor Card**
2. **Player Actions:** Starting with a randomly selected first player each round, each player takes one action:
   - **Pick Up:** Take the Floor Card (may discard one card from hand)
   - **Pass:** Decline the Floor Card
   - **Declare:** Declare a suit (if not already declared)
   - **Showdown:** Call for a Showdown (if minimum requirements are met)
3. **Unused Floor Cards:** If no player picks up the Floor Card, it goes to the bottom of the draw pile

**Dealer Rotation:** After each round, a new random player is selected to be the dealer. This ensures that no player repeatedly gains the advantage of being the first to act, and all players get an equal opportunity to experience different positions in the turn order over time.
